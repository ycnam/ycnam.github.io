홍익대 시각디자인과 졸업전시회의 영상작품상영 오프닝 영상. [전시 포스터](http://choihyemi.com/indexhibit/index.php?/projects/graduation-exhibition-poster-2008/)의 이미지의 그래픽 요소를 바탕으로 '영상'의 개념을 추상화하여 표현.

2008.

Opening title and Bridges of screening of Film works made by Students of Visual Communication Design Department, School of Design, Hong-ik University as graduation works. Abstract graphics based on the [poster](http://choihyemi.com/indexhibit/index.php?/projects/graduation-exhibition-poster-2008/) of the exhibition.
